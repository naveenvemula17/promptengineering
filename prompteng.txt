*** SHOT PROMPTING TECHNIQUE:

1.This technique is used for controlling ouput style and format without giving instructions.

2.To train the gpt model on a pattern without actual fine-tuning.

3.To improve accuracy on complex and structured tasks.

* ZERO SHOT PROMPTING:

we give no example but expect desired ans.

EG: sun rises in the east. 
ANS: yes.
but reply will not be crisp.

* ONE SHOT PROMPTING:

we give one example to show the pattern.

EG: sun rises in east. ANS : yes.

EG: sun sets in north.

--> Only few chatbots give support one shot prompting like grok.

* FEW SHOT PROMPTING:

1.we give 2-5 examples to understand the pattern,tone and format style.

-->Mostly all chatbots will support few shot prompting.


---------------------CHAIN OF THOUGHT -----------------

CoT prompting.

__zero shot CoT prompting.

   same as zero shot prompting but we will add prompt like think step by step.
   so that the machine bot will think carefully for some time.

   zero shot prompting     : A train travels at 120 kms for 2 hours,what's its speed?

   zero shot CoT prompting : A train travels at 120 kms for 2 hours,what's its speed? Think step by step.


Mostly used for arithmetic and reasoning questions.
